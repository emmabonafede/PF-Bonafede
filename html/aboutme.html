Hiya! I'm Emmanuel!

My name is Emmanuel, I'm 24 years old and I'm working and traveling throguhout the united states as an Au Pair in the State of California. I've been living here for a year and a half (almost) and i've had the opportunity to visit some of the most amazing places that we have around here. Wanna join me and travel together?!

To give you a bit of context, In the year 2015 i won a scolarship from Argentina (where I'm from) to live and study in Gatlinburg, Tennessee. So for the second half of 2016 i was living in the Smokey Mountains National Park and going to school at Gatlinburg Pittman High School (Go GP! Go Highlanders!). I was amazed by the culture, the buildings, the layout of the cities. It was so different than in Argentina where I used to live, that when my exchange ended I made it a goal of mine to come back to live in the States at some point.

Once back, I've studied Marketing at UCES (stands for Universidad de Ciencias Empresariales y Sociales) in Rafaela, Santa Fe. Once I was about to finish my studies, I enrolled myself in the Au pair program which would become a reality just about 6 months after. Now I'm living in Orange County, California. Just about an hour south of Hollywood and Los Angeles County! It's been a dream to live here, and I've been enjoying this state so much! It has a lot to do!!!! As you can see in my different tabs, I've traveled to Mexico, San Francisco, Tennessee, Las Vegas, Utah, and I have more trips coming soon to Yosemite, New York and my favorite, HAWAII!!
